--- layout: post title: "Sideways (3)" category: films ---

A fine film for the middle-aged man. I’m probably indulging in a bit of Schadenfreude as I’m no doubt happier and more confident in the direction my life is taking than our two unsuspecting protagonists, but I felt a good dose of empathy none the less. I also appreciated the plentiful use of wine-jargon; it felt like a vicarious trip to Napa.
