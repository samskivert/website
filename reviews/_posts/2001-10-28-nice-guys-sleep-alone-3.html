--- layout: post title: "Nice Guys Sleep Alone (3)" category: films ---

It’s a clear application of the “boy meets girl, complications arise, time passes, both parties see through complications and everything works out” formula which I normally avoid, but I had ulterior motives for seeing this film and having done so, I found it to be entertainment of exceptional quality. If you’re looking for a date film or if this sort of thing is your bag, you won’t be disappointed.
