--- layout: post title: "Like Shaking Hands with God – Kurt Vonnegut and Lee Stringer (3)" category: books ---

As the title claims, it’s a conversation about writing between these two writers. Vonnegut I’ve read and have a sympathy and apreciation for his take on life. Stringer I developed an instant respect for after reading his response to the first question posed in the conversation. I’ll be reading his work in the near future. The book is a worthwhile read for anyone pursuing or interested in writing, more so because it only takes an hour to read.
