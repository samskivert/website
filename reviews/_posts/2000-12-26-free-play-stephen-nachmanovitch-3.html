--- layout: post title: "Free Play – Stephen Nachmanovitch (3)" category: books ---

This period of my life is one of convergence. Not so much questions being answered as pieces falling into place and Free Play helped more pieces to fall into place. It’s a well executed treatise on creativity; creativity in many contexts and from many angles. It is both an inspiration to create and a helpful reminder of how to facilitate the process. The book is not lengthy, but not concise either. It comes recommended if you feel that a nudge toward creativity would do you good.
