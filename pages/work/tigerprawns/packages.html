Tigerprawns Package Design
Michael Bayne

This is an initial cut at a package breakdown of the components of the Tigerprawns system. The package assignments imply (at least at a high level) aspects of the design of the components. These may change as the design of the underlying components is further defined.

In deference to the de facto standard of packaging things with a prefix that identifies the developing organization, all packages will be prefixed with com.go2net.tigerprawns.

This means that we'll have package names like com.go2net.tigerprawns.util, which is verbose but definitely unique. Additionally, package names aren't frequently typed, so the negative effects of this verbosity are lessened.
