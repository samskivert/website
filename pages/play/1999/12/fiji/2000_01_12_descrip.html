today we headed out to hike up a coastal trail. this was far and away the biggest adventure we had the whole trip, but i'll let the pictures tell the tale.

the start of the trail was in a village and what did we find there but live chicken meat birds.

the trail went alongside the beach. it was strange to be covered in dense jungle only a few meters from the beach.
