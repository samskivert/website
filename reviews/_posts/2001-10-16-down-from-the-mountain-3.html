A fantastic documentary about the musicians that were brought together to write and perform the score for the Coen Brothers’ film O Brother, Where Art Thou? Anything but a group of narcissistic pop stars, these performers were the nicest group a’ people y’ever could meet. Humble, funny, hardworking musicians that demonstrated a love for their music and a gratitude that others loved it too. This film warms the ears as well as the cockles.
