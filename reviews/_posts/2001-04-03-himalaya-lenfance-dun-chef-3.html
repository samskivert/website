--- layout: post title: "Himalaya – L’Enfance D’Un Chef (3)" category: films ---

If you look up “visually stunning” in the Movie Reviewer’s Dictionary, you’ll find this movie. On top of the mind boggling scenery (and the yaks), the viewer is treated to a moving tale of tragedy, hardship and triumph that just couldn’t be delivered outside of the context of yak herders trekking across the Himalayas.
