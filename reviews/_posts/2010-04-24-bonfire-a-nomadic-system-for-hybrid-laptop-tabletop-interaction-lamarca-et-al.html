--- layout: post title: "Bonfire: A Nomadic System for Hybrid Laptop-Tabletop Interaction – LaMarca, et al." category: papers ---

Investigates what sort of fun one can have with a laptop, two micro-projectors, two video cameras and a whole lot of USB cabling and duct tape. The verdict: it’s not quite Minority Report, but you can do some neat stuff. Future WoW players will likely appreciate the auxiliary map and inventory displays.
