I’m a big fan of Akira Kurosawa’s films and High and Low did nothing but strengthen my convictions. Amazing shots from unlikely perspectives; a no-nonsense plot with bold, thought-provoking (if a bit cut and dry) moral issues; elegance and simplicity, yet depth. I do think that the antagonist was a bit strange, but he fit nicely in with the fifties propaganda film overtones.
