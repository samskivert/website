--- layout: post title: "Pokemon Puzzle Challenge – GBC (3)" category: games ---

The basic game idea is a lot of fun (sort of Tetris meets Connect 4 but not exactly). The variations on the theme are a lot of fun. Finding the hidden Pokemon is fun. The whole Pokemon schtick gets a wee bit tiring and the game is a little hard on one’s thumb, but if you like to have a few Game Boy games around that you can pick up and play while riding the train, this is a good choice.
