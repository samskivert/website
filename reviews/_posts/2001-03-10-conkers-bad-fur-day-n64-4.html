--- layout: post title: "Conker’s Bad Fur Day – N64 (4)" category: games ---

CBFD is a well executed game, soup to nuts. Even if it wasn’t chock full of scatological humor, brilliant movie parodies and tongue in cheek sexual innuendo, it would be a great platformer and a darned good adventure game. All things considered, it’s one of the best video games I’ve played since Monkey Island III. (Bear in mind that I’m a fan of platformers and adventure games, so you won’t find me raving about the latest FPS or RTS offerings.) It is shorter than your average game, but in these bountiful times, I count that as a blessing.
