fiji 2000

On december 16th 1999, myself and eighteen of my closest friends (well, actually eighteen of Paul, Jamie and my closest friends) hopped on an airplane destined for Nadi International Airport in Fiji. Sixteen more intrepid adventurers joined up along the way. Thirty days later, only five of us remained. This is the tale of our adventure (well, mine anyway).
